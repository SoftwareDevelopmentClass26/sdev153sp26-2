About the Series

The Legend of Zelda is a landmark action-adventure series created by Shigeru Miyamoto and Takashi Tezuka for Nintendo. Since its debut in 1986, it has become one of the most influential franchises in gaming history, known for its blend of exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat.

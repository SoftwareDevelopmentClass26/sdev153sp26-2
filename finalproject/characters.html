Main Characters

Link: Link is the primary protagonist of The Legend of Zelda series. He is typically depicted as a courageous young Hylian warrior who protects the Kingdom of Hyrule from malevolent forces, most notably Ganon. Throughout the games and lore, Link is not a single individual but a recurring soul that is reincarnated throughout history whenever Hyrule is in peril.

Princess Zelda: She is the eponymous deuteragonist of The Legend of Zelda series and the royal princess of the Kingdom of Hyrule. She is a core member of the series' central trio alongside Link and Ganon, and while she is frequently portrayed as a damsel in distress, she often serves as a powerful sage or magical ally who aids Link in his quests.

Ganon: Also known as Ganondorf in his human form, he is the primary antagonist of The Legend of Zelda series. He is an exceptionally powerful warlock and the Great King of Evil, destined to eternally oppose Link and Princess Zelda across time and multiple timelines.
